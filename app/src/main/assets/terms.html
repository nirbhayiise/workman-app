About IG3R Workman24-7

IG3R Workman24-7 is a creation of G3 Integrated Resources Services Ltd. It's a Mobile Application Software that provides professional Technical solutions as well as a platform for Service providers and customers to meet for various kinds of technical installations, maintenance, repairs, etc. At G3 Integrated Resources Services Ltd, our priority is efficient and flawless service delivery with our Customers at the core, driven by the passion to meet and exceed customer satisfaction. Our mission is to continuously improve on our systems and structures to ensure customer experience is assured.

Service Terms:

Every services request is handled within 24 hours by our professional Workmen, except emergencies. Any work that will exceed 24 hours is referred as MINI PROJECT and above 48 hours is referred as PROJECT.

Service Delivery Stages:

1. Requisition Stage: Customer makes request through 247Workman App or place a call to our SERVICE DESK 2. Supervision Stage: Services Administrator contacts the customer and agreed on the best time for inspection and service delivery. 3. Quotation & Payment Stage: Service quotation will be raised and send to the customer for payment before service delivery commence. 4. Job Close out Stage: Professional Workman (Technician) responsible for the services delivery will Sign Out and Close the completed job. 5. Customer Acceptance and Feedback Stage: Customer confirms that the requested service has been delivered and Sends feedback on his/her assessment about the Professional Workman and the services rendered.

Customer:

A registered person raises service request, receive quotation, make payment, receive service and confirm job completion

Requester:

A registered customer raise request on behalf of another customer e.g. spouse or children, groups (family, department or corporate organizations, e.t.c)

Service Provider:

IG3R Workman24-7 partners registered across various location to provide various categories of professional services. Professional Workman (Technicians): Screened and certified skilled workers who are assigned to work directly to resolve customer’s request.

ServiceDesk:

Provides 24/7 backend support to customers in case of any service difficulty.

System Administrator:

Superior technical and administrative backend support to all categories of users.
